placebear

A quick and simmple service for getting pictures of bears for use as placeholder in your design or code. Just put your image size (width & height) after our URL and You'll get a placeholder.

Like this: https://placebear.com/200/300
or: https://placebear.com/g/200/300
(Note: You can also add .jpg to the end of the URL, if you like.)
Note: Change the [width] & [height] and Check it!
