Dom Manipulation with Forms

This task involves creating a form to collect user information and displaying the submitted data in a table. The form includes fields for first name, last name, email, address, pincode, gender, choice of food (at least two out of five options), state, and country. On submission, the form data is appended to the table, and the form is cleared for new entries.
